Why I Left Google and Learned to Love Failure

October 20, 2011

I was invited to give a talk at the first annual PajamaConf this weekend and it was a blast. I spoke about my story of going to work for Google, having the time of my life, but realizing I wanted more and moving on. My goal was not to enlighten the audience about my life story, but rather to impart some wisdom I learned along the way.

I plan on writing more about this in the coming weeks, but for now here’s the short slide desk I prepared. Apparently people like it; it’s gone from 66 to 43,000+ views in about 24 hours on SlideShare. Enjoy!
